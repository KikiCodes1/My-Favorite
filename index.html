My Favorite hobby
Doing Art

My hobby is using my imagination and creativity. Whenever I am bored or sad, I like to draw or doodle. Using your imagination and creativity, you can do anything you put your mind to. Even if you are a grown-up, using your brain can lead to maybe even being a inventor! Exersising your brain could be very helpful for kids and adult's long life ahead of them. You do not do art for money, you do it for yourself. Just like all those great old and new artists out there. Art is awesome!
